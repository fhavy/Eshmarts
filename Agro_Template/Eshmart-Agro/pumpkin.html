Pumpkin Leaf

Pumpkin leaves are large, lobed leaves that grow on hollow stems. They are roundish in shape and often have serrated edges. They feature three or more veins. They are typically dark green but may be light or grey-green, depending on the variety. Pumpkin leaf, which is locally known as "Ugwu," is one of those plants that secretly carry some unbelievable health benefits. Pumpkin leaves have a healthy amount of Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Calcium, and Iron. And aside from the vision benefits of Vitamin A that the leaves provide, there are also numerous skin benefits.
